What We Do

  • Promote Firefox (Desktop and Android)
  • Support the successful launch of Firefox OS
  • Educate others about Mozilla's mission
  • Grow the Mozilla community
  • The Firefox Student Ambassador program empowers students with the tools, resources, and training to complete the below activities

    • Promote Firefox (Desktop and Android) through our Campus Affiliates Buttons
    • Support the successful launch of Firefox OS through the Mobilizer program, hosting App Days, and our App of the Month Contest
    • Grow the Mozilla Community through on-campus activities, events, social media promotion, and the work of our Regional Marketing Ambassadors
